TOGETHER FOR TOMORROW

It's truly inspiring to see TOMORROW X TOGETHER use their massive global platform for such a meaningful cause. Their TOGETHER FOR TOMORROW global campaign with UNICEF officially launched to focus entirely on youth and adolescent mental health. By openly breaking the stigma surrounding mental wellbeing, the members are directly aligning their philanthropic work with the core themes of empathy, growth, and resilience found in their music. Although they are not the foundation's first partners, TXT is unique in that their campaign has been created as a highly specialized program from the very first day, aimed at de-stigmatizing mental issues and fostering empathy. Instead of simply raising money, they create specific media platforms (such as the Secret Counseling Center show) where teenagers can openly discuss their fears.
