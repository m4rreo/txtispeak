Why I think TXT is so peak

Million selling albums

TOMORROW X TOGETHER has achieved the incredible milestone of 7 consecutive million-selling albums (selling over 1 million copies in the fisrt week on Hanteo). The new album, 7TH YEAR: A MOMENT OF STILLNESS IN THE THORNS surpassed the 1 million mark on its first day and its debut week sales totaled 1,806,740 copies.

Kings of stability

TXT is the only korean artist in history to have an album has charted on the Billboard 200 every year since their debut. They broke into the chart in 2019 with their debut EP, THE DREAM CHAPTER: STAR (which peaked at No. 140. Since then, the group has consistently placed their new releases on chart every year, from 2020 to 2026.

Leaders club

TXT joins Wanna One and ZEROBASEONE as the only K-pop group to achieve both the Rookie Grand Slam and Music Show Grand Slam. TOMORROW X TOGETHER have officially cemented their status as one of the most decorated and consistent groups of their generation.

From performers to headliners

In 2023, TXT became the first K-pop group to headline Lollapalooza in Chicago. A year earlier, in July 2022, TXT became the first K-pop group to simply perform at Lollapalooza (at the time, on a side stage). In 2023, they returned as full-fledged lineup leaders. The group's 2023 setlist consisted of 20 tracks, including live performances of the hits Good Boy Gone Bad, Sugar Rush Ride, and a special performance with American rapper Coi Leray.

Historical dominance in Japan

In April 2026, TOMORROW X TOGETHER became the first foreign artist in history to have 14 consecutive albums top the Oricon weekly chart . The group has consistently demonstrated high sales and strong fan support since their debut. This achievement confirms TXT's enormous popularity in the Japanese music market.

Global recognition

Yeonjun became the first Korean artist to participate in the official soundtrack for the 2026 World Baseball Classic, Make It Count, alongside international stars Becky G and Myke Towers. The tournament anthem was released on March 5, 2026. This is a historic event for the sports and music industries, as the International Baseball Federation released a full-length music album for the tournament for the first time.

Pioneers

The Weverse platform grew out of an exclusive fan community initially created just for TOMORROW X TOGETHER. So if you try to visit txtuniverse.com, you'll be redirected there. And it was a TXT member TAEHYUN who published the very first post in the network's history. In the summer of 2019, the TXT community's work officially transformed into the global Weverse platform, and TXT themselves became forever known as the network's very first artists. This site became the official testing ground for all the features of the future app. Fans loved this format so much that the company undertook a major rebranding.
