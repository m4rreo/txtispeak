Pioneers

The Weverse platform grew out of an exclusive fan community initially created just for TOMORROW X TOGETHER. So if you try to visit txtuniverse.com, you'll be redirected there. And it was a TXT member Taehyun who published the very first post in the network's history. In the summer of 2019, the TXT community's work officially transformed into the global Weverse platform, and TXT themselves became forever known as the network's very first artists. This site became the official testing ground for all the features of the future app. Fans loved this format so much that the company undertook a major rebranding.

Unique sound

TXT's musical variety is one of the main reasons why their discography is considered one of the best in the fourth generation of K-pop. Each of their comebacks isn't just a new song, but an entire conceptual story told through a completely new musical genre.

Voice of a generation

TOMORROW X TOGETHER universe is widely praised for its cinematic depth. At its core, the lore follows five boys who make a promise to a star to always return to each other. Each member embodies a specific trait (Hope, Promise, Clue, Tomorrow, and Secret), and their struggles center on identity, friendship, and holding onto hope in a cynical, deteriorating reality. Over the years, the storyline has expanded into multiple formats, offering different ways to experience the narrative like music videos and webtoons.
